Login or sign up at office.com/setup and download Microsoft Office. Install and activate the setup on your device.


Microsoft Office provides a suite of applications that cater to various needs. Office Setup refers to the process of downloading, installing, and configuring Microsoft Office on your computer or device. A unique 25-character code provided with your Office purchase to setup Office at office.com/setup. An office can be installed in almost every digital smart device, be it phone, laptop, tablet, and desktop. In case you are already an existing user and are using an older version of Office, then you can upgrade your existing Office program by navigating to the www.office.com/setup.


Office Setup Product Key

Office setup product key is a 25-character code (XXXXX-XXXXX-XXXXX-XXXXX-XXXXX) including letters and numbers used to activate Microsoft Office products. Enter the product key during the Office setup process to activate your product.


Create a Microsoft Account

  • Go to office.com/setup or www.office.com/setup.
  • Click on "Create a Microsoft account".
  • Enter your:
    • Email address (or phone number)
    • Password
    • First and last name
  • Add a recovery email or phone number.
  • Enter your birthdate and country/region.
  • Review the terms of service and privacy statement.
  • Click "Next" to create your account.

How to download and install office setup?

Download Office Setup:

  • Go to "Office.com/setup".
  • Sign in with your Microsoft account.
  • Enter your product key (if prompted).
  • Select your Office version (e.g., Office 365, Office 2019).
  • Choose your language and installation options.
  • Click "Download" to download the Office setup file.

Install Office Setup:

  • Run the downloaded Office setup file.
  • Click "Yes" to allow the installation.
  • Follow the installation prompts.
  • Accept the terms and conditions.
  • Choose the installation location (optional).
  • Click "Install" to begin the installation.
  • Wait for the installation to complete.
  • Click "Close" to finish the installation.

Activating Office setup

  • After installation completes, open the program.
  • Launch it and simply visit Activate section.
  • Press “Activate” product button and wait till the wizard appears on the screen.
  • Select Microsoft Office activation when it prompts.
  • Here, simply click on the “Next” option which is available on-screen.
  • Now, you will come across the field where the product activation code or key is to be typed.
  • Type the key or numbers in requested blank and follow all instructions given on-screen.
  • This will help in activating Office product on a device easily.
